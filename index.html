About Us

Arami Drinks & Juice Bar is a trusted beverage manufacturer in Eleme, Rivers State, dedicated to bringing you the finest selection of natural drinks and freshly squeezed juices. From our facility at Kembere Junction Ekara Onne, we source quality local fruits and ingredients to create beverages that are not just delicious, but nutritious. Our commitment is simple: no artificial preservatives, no added sugars unless requested, just pure goodness in every bottle. Whether you're looking for a quick refreshment or bulk orders for events and retail, Arami is your go-to destination for quality you can taste.
